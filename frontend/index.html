Ascend

A research-grade platform that navigates emotional states through music — not playlists, but curated trajectories through continuous Valence-Arousal space. Rather than static mood categories, Ascend maps your emotional journey as a mathematical path and selects tracks that guide you from where you are to where you want to be.

WHAT IT DOES
Transitions your emotional state using music selected along a computed VA trajectory.
HOW IT WORKS
Place your current and target mood on the dimensional plane. The engine generates a path and retrieves tracks via FAISS nearest-neighbor search.
WHY IT'S DIFFERENT
No genre tags. No mood labels. Pure dimensional psychology — Valence, Arousal, and real-time adaptive personalisation.
